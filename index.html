The Uneven Landscape of Blood Donations and Organ Pledges in Malaysia

This visualisation explores how blood donation and organ pledge participation differ across Malaysian states between 2010 and 2025. Through maps, temporal trends, and comparative visualisations, the story reveals where healthcare participation is concentrated, how participation changes over time, and why willingness to pledge does not always translate into actual donations.

Participation is Concentrated in Urban and Developed States

Blood donation and organ pledge activity is heavily concentrated in highly urbanised states such as Selangor and Kuala Lumpur, while several eastern and northern states contribute substantially less.
